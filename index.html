About Me

Growing up in South Florida, I've always had a bigger vision than what's around me. Day after day, I wake up and see sugar cane being harvested along side other agriculture areas and thought to myself; "I want to do something bigger in life."

After taking one coding class of python, I knew this was something that I enjoy and could see myself doing for many years to come. If not the rest of my life.

My vision seems to be very far away, But this is the first step in the right direction.
